Choose Your Path

Our college offers a wide range of undergraduate programs across different fields including Technology, Science, Commerce, Arts, and Management to provide diverse career opportunities for students.

B.Tech → Career Options

After completing B.Tech, students can work as Software Developers, Engineers, or IT Professionals. They can also go into fields like Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Cyber Security. Many students pursue M.Tech or MBA for higher studies. 💰 Average Salary: ₹4–12 LPA depending on skills and company.

BCA → Career Options

After BCA, students can become Web Developers, Software Engineers, or Database Administrators. They can also specialize in mobile app development and cloud computing. Many students go for MCA for advanced knowledge. 💰 Average Salary: ₹3–8 LPA with good growth in IT sector.

BBA → Career Options

After BBA, students can work as Business Managers, Marketing Executives, or HR Managers. They can join companies or start their own business. Many students pursue MBA for better opportunities. 💰 Average Salary: ₹3–10 LPA depending on role and experience.

B.Sc → Career Options

After B.Sc, students can go into research, teaching, or technical fields. They can work as Lab Technicians, Scientists, or Research Assistants. Many pursue M.Sc or competitive exams. 💰 Average Salary: ₹2–6 LPA initially, increases with higher studies.

B.A → Career Options

After B.A, students can prepare for civil services, teaching, media, or government jobs. They can also pursue M.A or professional courses. 💰 Average Salary: ₹2–7 LPA depending on field and job type.

B.Com → Career Options

After B.Com, students can work in accounting, banking, and finance sectors. They can become Accountants or Financial Analysts. Many pursue CA, CS, or MBA. 💰 Average Salary: ₹3–9 LPA depending on qualification.

MBA → Career Options

After MBA, students can take managerial roles like Business Manager, Marketing Head, or Operations Manager. They can work in top companies or start their own business. 💰 Average Salary: ₹6–20 LPA depending on college and specialization.

LLB → Career Options

After LLB, students can become Lawyers, Legal Advisors, or Judges. They can work in courts, law firms, or corporate sectors.💰 Average Salary: ₹3–10 LPA, higher with experience and practice.

B.Sc Nursing → Career Options

After B.Sc Nursing, students can work as Nurses or Healthcare Professionals in hospitals and clinics. They can also go abroad for better opportunities.💰 Average Salary: ₹3–7 LPA in India, higher abroad.

AI & Data Science → Career Options

After this course, students can become Data Scientists, AI Engineers, or ML Experts. They can work in tech companies and startups. 💰 Average Salary: ₹6–25 LPA due to high demand in this field.
