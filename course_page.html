Courses We Offer

Our college offers a wide range of undergraduate programs across different fields including Technology, Science, Commerce, Arts, and Management to provide diverse career opportunities for students.

B.Tech

B.Tech is a 4-year undergraduate engineering program that focuses on technical knowledge, programming, and problem-solving skills. Students learn subjects like computer science, electronics, and mechanical systems. It prepares students for careers in IT, software development, and core engineering industries. The course also includes practical labs and project-based learning to build real-world skills.

BCA

BCA is a 3-year course designed for students interested in computer applications and IT fields. It covers programming languages, database management, web development, and software engineering basics. Students gain hands-on experience in coding and application development. It is a great option for those who want to build a career in software and technology sectors.

BBA

BBA is a professional course that focuses on business management, marketing, finance, and human resources. It helps students develop leadership, communication, and decision-making skills. The course prepares students for corporate jobs and managerial roles. It is also a strong foundation for pursuing higher studies like MBA.

B.Sc

B.Sc is a science-based undergraduate program that includes subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. It builds a strong foundation in scientific knowledge and analytical thinking. Students can explore research opportunities and higher education. It is suitable for careers in teaching, research, and technical fields.

B.A

B.A is a humanities-based course that includes subjects like History, Political Science, Sociology, and English. It helps students develop critical thinking, communication, and analytical skills. The course is useful for careers in civil services, teaching, and media. It also provides a broad understanding of society and culture.

B.Com

B.Com focuses on commerce subjects such as accounting, finance, taxation, and business law. It provides strong knowledge of financial systems and business operations. Students can pursue careers in banking, accounting, and finance sectors. It is also a good base for professional courses like CA and MBA.

MBA

MBA is a postgraduate program that develops advanced business and management skills. It includes subjects like marketing, finance, operations, and leadership. The course prepares students for high-level managerial roles in companies. It also helps in developing entrepreneurship and business strategies.

LLB

LLB is a law degree that provides knowledge about legal systems, rules, and regulations. Students study subjects like constitutional law, criminal law, and corporate law. It prepares them for careers as lawyers, judges, and legal advisors. Practical training and case studies are important parts of this course.

B.Sc Nursing

B.Sc Nursing is a healthcare program focused on patient care, medical knowledge, and hospital practices. Students learn about anatomy, physiology, and clinical procedures. The course prepares them to work in hospitals, clinics, and healthcare organizations. It is a highly respected and responsible profession.

AI & Data Science

This modern course focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science technologies. Students learn programming, data analysis, and AI tools. It prepares them for future careers in tech industries. The demand for AI professionals is growing rapidly worldwide.
