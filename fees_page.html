Scholarship Program

Our college provides various scholarship opportunities to support talented and deserving students. Scholarships are offered based on academic performance, financial background, and special categories.

Merit-Based Scholarship

Students who score above 85% in their previous exams can get up to 50% fee waiver.

Need-Based Scholarship

Financially weak students can apply for special support with required documents.

Government Scholarship

Students can also apply for state and central government scholarships as per eligibility.

Sports Scholarship

Students with excellent performance in sports can get special benefits and fee discounts.
